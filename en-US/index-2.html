ABOUT US

Doruk Group

Doruk Group which was established in 2000, Ankara appears in the global market with Guillotine Systems, Bioclimatic, Pergolas, Wind Breaker, Sliding Glass Systems, Zip Blinds, Wintend, Glass Ceilling and Panoramic Glass systems. All metal and plastic components of these projects are produced in their own facilities. In addition, it strengthens its position in the sector with its R&D studies, responds to the increasing needs of our customers and pioneers innovations in the sector by constantly developing new products.
