About Us
Doruk Group which was established in 2000, Ankara appears in the global market with Pergola, Bioclimatic, Guillotine, Windbreaker, Glazing Systems, Zip Curtain and Wintend systems products. All metal and plastic components of these projects are produced in their own facilities. In addition, it strengthens its position in the sector with its R&D studies, responds to the increasing needs of our customers and pioneers innovations in the sector by constantly developing new products.
